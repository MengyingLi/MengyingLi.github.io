I'm a Data Scientist at WDG (Windows Device Group) Core Data Science Team of Microsoft. I joined Microsoft right afte graduating from Columbia University with a M.A. degree in Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences.
I am specialized in data analysis and data visualization. I am familiar with R, Python, U-SQL, Stata, ArcGIS, HTML, CSS and JavaScript (D3.js, Leaflet.js, etc.). My current projects at Microsoft focus on app usage, app install base, Windows gaming population and device hardware attributes. I enjoy going to data science and data visualization meetups in my free time. I maintain some of my projects on GitHub.
